## What to Look for in Junk Removal Software

Before diving into specific platforms, understand what features matter most:

### Core Features

- **Online booking**: Let customers schedule without calling
- **Dispatch management**: Assign jobs to trucks and drivers efficiently
- **GPS tracking**: Know where your trucks are in real-time
- **Invoicing and payments**: Process cards on-site or send invoices
- **Customer management**: Store contact info, job history, and notes
- **Reporting**: Track revenue, profitability, and job metrics

### Nice-to-Have Features

- **Route optimization**: Automatically plan efficient routes
- **Photo estimation**: Quote jobs from customer photos
- **Automated follow-ups**: Send review requests and thank-you messages
- **Inventory tracking**: Monitor disposal costs and recycling
- **Multi-location support**: Manage multiple markets or franchises
- **Integration options**: Connect with QuickBooks, Google Calendar, etc.

### Pricing Considerations

Software pricing typically falls into three models:

1. **Per-user/month**: Pay for each team member using the system
2. **Flat-rate monthly**: One price regardless of users
3. **Percentage of revenue**: Pay a % of jobs processed through the platform

Consider both upfront costs and long-term scalability. A cheap solution that doesn't grow with you becomes expensive when you need to migrate later.

## The Hidden Costs of Choosing Wrong

Beyond monthly fees, consider the total cost of ownership:

- **Integration needs**: Does it connect with your accounting software?
- **Support quality**: Can you get help when you need it?

Choosing the cheapest option often leads to switching costs down the road. The best software is the one that grows with your business without forcing a painful migration.

## Questions to Ask Before Buying

1. **Can I try it free?** Most platforms offer 14-30 day trials—test before committing
2. **What's included in the base price?** Watch for add-on fees (payment processing, route optimization, etc.)
3. **How does pricing change as I grow?** Per-user fees can explode as you scale
4. **What's the contract term?** Month-to-month offers flexibility; annual saves money but locks you in
5. **Is implementation included?** Some charge thousands for setup and training
6. **What does support cost?** Email-only vs. phone/chat makes a difference
7. **Can I export my data?** Never get trapped—ensure you can leave if needed
