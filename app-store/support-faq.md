### Service & Safety

**Q: Are your haulers licensed and insured?**  
A: Absolutely. All haulers on the Umuve platform are vetted, licensed, and carry liability insurance. We perform background checks and verify credentials before approving any service provider.

**Q: Can I track my hauler?**  
A: Yes! You'll receive real-time tracking once your hauler is on the way. See their location, estimated arrival time, and driver information in the app.

**Q: What if the hauler is late or doesn't show up?**  
A: We take punctuality seriously. If your hauler is running late, you'll receive a notification with an updated ETA. If they're more than 30 minutes late without notice, contact support immediately for priority assistance or rebooking at no charge.

**Q: What happens to my items after pickup?**  
A: We prioritize eco-friendly disposal. Usable items are donated to local charities, recyclable materials go to proper recycling facilities, and remaining waste is disposed of responsibly. You'll see a disposal summary in your completion receipt.

## Common Issues & Solutions

### Issue: "Upload Failed" when submitting photos

**Solutions:**
- Check your internet connection (switch between WiFi/cellular)
- Reduce photo file size: tap "Optimize Photos" before uploading
- Close and reopen the app
- Try uploading one photo at a time instead of multiple
- Contact support if problem persists

---

### Issue: No available time slots showing

**Solutions:**
- Try expanding your search window (select different days)
- Check if you're searching during peak times (weekends fill up fast)
- Enable "Notify Me" to get alerts when new slots open
- Consider booking further in advance for guaranteed availability
- Contact support for priority scheduling assistance

---

### Issue: Price seems too high/low
