## Description

Book professional junk removal in South Florida — fast, affordable, and eco-friendly.

JunkOS connects you with trusted haulers who handle everything from single-item pickups to full property cleanouts. Snap photos of what needs to go, pick a time that works, and we'll take care of the rest.

KEY FEATURES

- Photo-Based Quoting: Take photos of your junk and get an instant estimate. No guesswork.
- Flexible Scheduling: Choose from morning, afternoon, or evening time slots that fit your schedule.
- Full-Service or Curbside: In-home pickup with heavy lifting, or save 25% with curbside drop-off.
- Eco-Friendly Disposal: We donate usable items and recycle what we can. 60% landfill diversion rate.
- Secure Payment: Pay with Apple Pay or credit card. Your payment info is encrypted and never stored.
- Real-Time Updates: Get notifications when your hauler is on the way and when the job is complete.
- Commercial & Recurring: Bulk pricing for businesses with weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly pickups.
- Property Cleanouts: Estate, foreclosure, and eviction cleanout specialists.

SERVICES WE OFFER

- Furniture Removal (couches, mattresses, desks, tables)
- Appliance Disposal (refrigerators, washers, dryers)
- Electronics Recycling (TVs, computers, printers)
- Yard Waste Cleanup (branches, leaves, lawn debris)
- Construction Debris (drywall, lumber, tiles)
- Full Property Cleanouts

HOW IT WORKS

1. Choose your service and enter your address
2. Upload photos so we can provide an accurate quote
3. Pick a date and time that works for you
4. Confirm and pay securely through the app
5. Our insured team arrives, loads everything, and hauls it away

Serving Miami-Dade, Broward, and Palm Beach counties.
