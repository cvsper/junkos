## Troubleshooting

### Can't Log In

**Problem**: "Invalid email or password"

**Solutions**:
- Double-check your email address (no typos)
- Ensure caps lock is off for password
- Try resetting password: Tap "Forgot Password?"
- Contact your operator if invitation email expired

---

### Not Receiving Job Notifications

**Problem**: You don't see alerts for new jobs

**Solutions**:
1. Check notifications are enabled (see [Step 3](#enable-notifications))
2. Verify you're marked as "Available" in settings
3. Check your phone's Do Not Disturb is off
4. Ensure app is updated to latest version
5. Log out and log back in

---

### GPS/Navigation Not Working

**Problem**: Can't get directions or location tracking fails

**Solutions**:
1. Enable location permissions (see [Step 3](#enable-location-services))
2. Make sure Location Services are on in phone settings
3. Check you have internet connection (WiFi or mobile data)
4. Try restarting the app
5. Restart your phone

---

### Photos Won't Upload

**Problem**: "Failed to upload photo" error

**Solutions**:
1. Check internet connection (WiFi or data)
2. Ensure you have storage space on your phone
3. Try closing other apps to free up memory
4. Take photo again with better lighting
5. If persistent, save photos to gallery and upload later from job details

---

### App Crashes or Freezes

**Problem**: App closes unexpectedly or becomes unresponsive

**Solutions**:
1. **Update app**: Check App Store/Play Store for updates
2. **Restart app**: Close completely and reopen
3. **Restart phone**: Power off and back on
4. **Clear cache** (Android): Settings ‚Üí Apps ‚Üí JunkOS ‚Üí Clear Cache
5. **Reinstall app**: Delete and download again (won't lose data)
6. Report issue to support@junkos.app with:
   - Phone model and OS version
   - What you were doing when it crashed

---

### Payment Collection Issues

**Problem**: Card reader not connecting or payment declined

**Solutions**:

**Card reader not connecting**:
- Turn Bluetooth on in phone settings
- Restart card reader (hold button for 5 seconds)
- Re-pair device: Settings ‚Üí Payment ‚Üí Card Reader ‚Üí Pair New Device

**Payment declined**:
- Ask customer to use different card
- Verify card number was entered correctly
- Decline message shows reason (insufficient funds, expired, etc.)
- Customer should contact their bank if unclear

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I get paid?

Your operator handles driver payroll. Payment schedules vary by company (weekly, bi-weekly, etc.). Check with your operator for specifics.

### Can I see my earnings in the app?

Yes! Go to **Settings** ‚Üí **"Earnings"** to view:
- Today's earnings
- This week/month total
- Payment history
- Breakdown by job

### What if the customer isn't home?

1. Call the customer (tap phone icon in job details)
2. Wait 10-15 minutes
3. If no answer, message your operator
4. Your operator will advise: wait longer, reschedule, or cancel

### Can I accept/decline job assignments?

This depends on your company's policy:
- Some operators auto-assign (you're expected to accept)
- Others let you accept/decline within a time window
- Check your company's specific rules

### What if I damage something?

1. **Stop and assess**: Make sure customer and you are safe
2. **Inform the customer** immediately: Don't hide it
3. **Document**: Take photos of the damage
4. **Report to operator**: Use in-app message or call
5. **File incident report**: In app under job notes

Your operator will handle insurance claims.

### Can I take tips?

Most companies allow tips! Your operator's policy may vary. If a customer wants to tip:
- **Cash tips**: Typically yours to keep
- **In-app tips**: May be included in your pay or split per company policy

Check your company's tipping policy.

### What items can/can't I take?

**Generally accepted**:
- Furniture, appliances (non-working okay)
- Yard waste, construction debris
- Electronics (TVs, computers)
- Household items

**Generally prohibited**:
- Hazardous materials (paint, chemicals, asbestos)
- Medical waste
- Firearms, ammunition
- Perishable food waste (in some areas)

**If unsure**, message your operator for guidance. Never take prohibited items‚Äîpenalties can be severe.
