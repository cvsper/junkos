# Umuve Operator Onboarding Video Script

**Duration**: ~5 minutes  
**Target Audience**: New junk removal operators  
**Tone**: Friendly, professional, concise  
**Format**: Screen recording with voiceover

---

## Pre-Production Checklist

- [ ] Set up demo account with sample data
- [ ] Prepare test customer and driver accounts
- [ ] Clear browser cache for clean UI
- [ ] Set screen resolution to 1920x1080
- [ ] Close unnecessary windows/tabs
- [ ] Disable notifications during recording
- [ ] Test audio levels
- [ ] Prepare cursor highlighting (if using)

---

## Scene Breakdown

### INTRO (0:00 - 0:25)

#### Visual
- Umuve logo animation
- Fade to operator dashboard with "Welcome" overlay

#### Narration
> "Welcome to Umuve, the complete operating system for junk removal businesses. In just five minutes, I'll show you everything you need to start accepting bookings and managing jobs. Let's get started."

#### Recording Notes
- Use upbeat background music (fade under narration)
- Keep logo on screen for 3 seconds
- Smooth fade to dashboard

---

### SECTION 1: Dashboard Overview (0:25 - 1:00)

#### Visual
**[Screen: Dashboard home page]**
- Cursor highlights each section as mentioned

#### Narration
> "This is your command center. On the left, you've got your main navigation: Jobs, Calendar, Drivers, Customers, and Invoices. The dashboard shows today's activity at a glance: active jobs, completed jobs, today's revenue, and your driver status. Everything you need to run your operation is right here."

#### Recording Notes
- **0:25**: Hover over sidebar items (don't click)
- **0:35**: Circle cursor around "Today's Overview" widget
- **0:45**: Point to driver status widget
- **0:50**: Pause on dashboard for 2 seconds

### SECTION 2: Creating a Job (1:00 - 2:10)

#### Visual
**[Screen: New job creation flow]**

#### Narration (Part 1)
> "Let's create your first job. Click the 'New Job' button. First, enter your customer's information—name, phone, email, and service address."

#### Recording Notes
- **1:00**: Click "+ New Job" button
- **1:08**: Fill in customer form:
  - Name: "John Smith"
  - Phone: "(555) 123-4567"
  - Email: "john@example.com"
  - Address: "123 Main St, Anytown, ST 12345"
- **1:20**: Click "Continue" or scroll to next section

#### Narration (Part 2)
> "Next, select the service type. Let's choose 'Full-Service Junk Removal.' Add the items you'll be removing—in this case, a couch and mattress. Estimate the volume, about a quarter truck load."

#### Recording Notes
- **1:28**: Click "Service Type" dropdown
- **1:32**: Select "Full-Service Junk Removal"
- **1:38**: Click "Add Items"
- **1:40**: Type "Couch" and hit enter
- **1:42**: Type "Mattress" and hit enter
- **1:46**: Select "1/4 Truck Load" from volume dropdown
- **1:50**: Show auto-calculated price ($179)

#### Narration (Part 3)
> "Pick a date and time window. Umuve automatically calculates pricing based on volume. Assign a driver now or do it later. Click 'Create Job' and you're done!"

#### Recording Notes
- **1:55**: Click date picker, select tomorrow's date
- **1:58**: Select time window "10:00 AM - 12:00 PM"
- **2:02**: Click "Assign Driver" dropdown, select "Mike Johnson"
- **2:06**: Click "Create Job" button
- **2:08**: Show success message

#### Visual
**[Screen: Job list → Job detail page]**

### SECTION 5: Invoicing & Payments (3:35 - 4:20)

#### Visual
**[Screen: Invoices page → Payment settings]**

#### Narration (Part 1)
> "Let's talk about getting paid. Go to Invoices to see all your billing. Invoices are automatically created when jobs are completed. Your customers receive a link to pay online with credit card, debit card, or bank transfer."

#### Recording Notes
- **3:35**: Click "Invoices" in sidebar
- **3:40**: Show invoice list
- **3:45**: Click on an invoice
- **3:48**: Show invoice details with payment link
- **3:52**: Point to payment status

#### Narration (Part 2)
> "To receive payments, you need to connect your bank account via Stripe. Just go to Settings, click Payments, and click 'Connect with Stripe.' It takes about three minutes. Once connected, payments go directly to your bank account on your chosen schedule—daily, weekly, or monthly."

#### Recording Notes
- **4:00**: Click profile icon → Settings
- **4:04**: Click "Payments" tab
- **4:08**: Show Stripe Connect button (or "Connected" status if already set up)
- **4:12**: Point to payout schedule settings
- **4:16**: Show example: "Next payout: $1,847 on Feb 15"
