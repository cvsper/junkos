What is Umuve?

Umuve is South Florida's on-demand junk removal and hauling platform connecting customers with 500+ vetted operators across Palm Beach and Broward counties. We handle furniture removal, appliance disposal, estate cleanouts, and construction debris with same-day service and upfront pricing.

Unlike traditional junk removal companies, Umuve operates as a technology platform—matching jobs to the nearest available operator within 30 seconds for 47% faster service. Customers get instant online quotes (no phone calls required), real-time tracking, and eco-friendly disposal with 87% diversion from landfills.
