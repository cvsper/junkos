Frequently Asked Questions

What are the requirements to join?

You need a truck (pickup or box truck), valid driver's license, insurance, and ability to lift heavy items. We accept operators with 1-3 trucks.

How much do I earn per job?

You keep 85% of every job. Average job in South Florida is $250, so you'd earn $212.50. Jobs range from $100-$800 depending on volume.

When do I get paid?

Same day. Once you mark a job complete, payment is automatically deposited to your bank account within hours.

Can I set my own schedule?

Yes. You choose which jobs to accept based on your availability. No minimum hours required.

What if a customer cancels?

If they cancel less than 24 hours before pickup, you still get a $50 cancellation fee. We protect your time.

Do I need my own insurance?

Yes, you need commercial auto and general liability insurance. We provide additional coverage through the platform as a backup.
