# Umuve Privacy Policy (App Store Summary)

**Last Updated:** February 7, 2026

## What We Collect

- **Contact Info:** Name, email, phone number
- **Location:** Used only during booking to verify service area and pickup location (not stored permanently)
- **Photos:** Images of items to remove (deleted after service completion)
- **Address:** Service location for junk removal
- **Booking History:** Your service requests and completed jobs (retained 7 years for compliance)
- **Payment Info:** Processed securely via Stripe (when available)

## How We Use Your Data

- Provide junk removal services
- Calculate quotes and schedule pickups
- Send booking confirmations and updates
- Customer support
- Legal compliance

## Data Sharing

- **We never sell your data**
- Shared only with: our backend server, Stripe (payments), and SMS/email services (notifications)
- All providers are required to protect your information

## Your Rights

- **Access** your data anytime
- **Delete** your account in app settings
- **Opt out** of marketing emails/SMS
- **California residents:** Full CCPA rights (right to know, delete, opt-out)
- **EU residents:** Full GDPR rights

## Data Retention

- Photos: Deleted within 30 days after service
- Booking data: 7 years (legal requirement)
- Location: Not stored permanently
- Account data: Until you delete your account

## Security

We use encryption (TLS/SSL) and industry-standard security practices to protect your information.
